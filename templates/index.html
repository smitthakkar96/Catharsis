Why do we do what we do?

Catharsis, a process of releasing, and thereby providing relief from,strong and repressed emotions. Depression, Suicide, Anxiety..Mental disorders. In a world where physical diseases are talked about in the open, mental disorders are judged and detested. Here, in Catharsis, we are trying to solve and bring light to the issue of mental health. Virginia Woolfe, Tom Wills, Robin Williams, approximately 1 million people commit suicide every year. 350 million suffer from depression. If only these people could vent their repressed emotions, then maybe..well, maybe we wouldn still have them with us. Suicide takes place in a matter of seconds, and what we need to do is talk to them in those seconds,for it could save their lives.
